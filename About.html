About us

Founded in 2005, Hak5's mission is to advance the InfoSec industry. We do this through our award winning podcasts, leading pentest gear, and inclusive community – where all hackers belong.

Hak5 gear have found their way into the hearts and tool-kits of enthusiasts and red-teams alike. They're notable for being effective, easy and expandable. Our design philosophy is simple – make it do the thing. From WiFi audits to covert implants and hotplug mayhem, Hak5 gear delivers
